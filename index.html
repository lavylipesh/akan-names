AKAN NAMES

The Akan population of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.

These names are derived from the Ghananian culture.

These is an application that will calculate the day of the week that someone was born depending on their date of birth and give them an akan name based on their gender.
